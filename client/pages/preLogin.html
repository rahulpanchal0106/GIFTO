Welcome to GIFTO

GIFTO is a community engagement platform designed to foster connections and empower individuals and businesses in GIFT City. Our mission is to create a thriving, inclusive, and forward-thinking community.

For Residents

Stay updated on events, connect with neighbors, and contribute to shaping our city's future.

For Workers

Explore professional opportunities, network with peers, and be an active part of GIFT City's growth.

For Businesses

Promote your services, connect with customers, and be a key player in our local economy.

Benefits of Joining GIFTO

  • Access to exclusive events and workshops
  • Networking opportunities with fellow residents and professionals
  • Contribute to shaping the future of GIFT City through your ideas and feedback
  • Promote your business and services to a local audience
